# Terra Terminalis FAQ

## What is Terra Terminalis?

A command-based notebook system inspired by analog notebooks and built for digital use through the terminal.

---

## Is this a subscription?

No.

Terra Terminalis is built around ownership, not recurring fees.

---

## Who is this for?

People who work well with systems, plain text, structure, and direct control.

---

## Do I need Linux experience?

Basic terminal comfort helps, but the system is designed to remain practical and usable.

---

## What do the modules do?

Pocket = Capture  
Iter = Analyze  
Atlas = Build  
Ledger = Track

---

## Is this for everyone?

No.

It is intentionally built for a narrower type of user who values discipline and structure.

---

## How is it delivered?

Digital access after purchase.

---

## Will it be updated?

Yes. Improvements and refinements may be released over time.
